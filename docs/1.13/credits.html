Credits

SpyDrNet was first conceived by Andrew Keller, Dr. Michael Wirthlin, and some additional students. They envisioned a next generation tool for the analysis and transformation of netlists in many different formats for many different applications. The tool follows its predecessor, BYU EDIF Tools. Many persons, organizations, grants, and other entities have contributed (or currently contribute) to the development, documentation, improvement, and design of SpyDrNet.
